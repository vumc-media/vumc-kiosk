VUMC Kids Check-In

Use this page to open the VUMC Kids Check-In form for families and children. This keeps the process simple, quick, and easy to access from Ministry Connect.

How this works

  • Tap the Kids Check-In button.
  • The Church Center form opens in a modal.
  • Complete the check-in details for the child or family.
  • Close the modal when finished to return here.
